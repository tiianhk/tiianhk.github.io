About

I am working with Dr Charalampos Saitis and Dr Stefan Lattner on music/timbre representations, neural audio synthesis, and creative AI tools for music. Previously I received my Master's degree in Music Technology at NYU and I was a research intern at Xiaohongshu (RedNote).
